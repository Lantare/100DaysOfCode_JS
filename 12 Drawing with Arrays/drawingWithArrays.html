How it works
Create a pixel drawing by writing it out in the format (colour_code colour_code....break).
The colour_codes available currently can be seen in the list below.
Add break where you want a row to end.

For example:
BL W BL W BL break
W BL W BL W break
BL W BL W BL break
W BL W BL W break
BL W BL W BL break
 Will give a simple 5x5 black and white chessboard.

Some pre-defined drawings can be found at the bottom of the page.
    List of current colours
  • Orange - O
  • Grey - G
  • Red - R
  • Black - BL
  • White - W
  • Bright Green - B_GR
  • Light Green - L_GR
  • Light Blue - L_B
  • Brown - BR
